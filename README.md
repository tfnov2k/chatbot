# Govt Assist Chatbot

The Government of Mauritius's virtual assistant is designed in a way that improves citizen experience on the national website. This chatbot is AI-powered and serves as a digital guide. Its aim is to help Mauritians navigate government services across technology, infrastructure and healthcare sectors. Govt-Assist Chatbot responds to a wide range of inquiries intelligently to provide personalized support to citizens who are looking for information or assistance with government services. The chatbot uses advanced artificial intelligence together with some pre-determined conditions to understand user needs. It then delivers relevant and accurate responses for each individual's specific situation.
